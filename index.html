    I am Rafiq Ur Rehman, a dedicated and passionate chemical engineer with a strong focus on materials science, electrochemistry, and renewable energy technologies. My academic journey, culminating in a Master's degree from the National University of Sciences & Technology (NUST), has equipped me with deep expertise in developing advanced electrocatalysts for sustainable energy applications. My research primarily centers on innovative water-splitting technologies, where I have contributed to optimizing hybrid materials for enhanced energy conversion. With hands-on experience in material synthesis, electrochemical analysis, and catalysis, I aim to drive cutting-edge solutions in the renewable energy sector. Through my work, I am committed to addressing global energy challenges by leveraging science and technology for a cleaner, more sustainable future.
